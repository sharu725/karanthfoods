Technology

Vacuum frying is a process of cooking food items under reduced atmospheric pressure, typically in a vacuum chamber. This method is an alternative to traditional deep frying and involves frying at lower temperatures.

  • Vacuum Frying

    Vacuum frying at lower temperatures minimizes oxidation, reducing exposure to the atmosphere.

  • Healthy

    Fruits and veggies that undergo vacuum frying preserve the natural nutritional content of the original fruits and vegetables.

  • Natural

    Fruit Treat products maintain their natural colour, flavour, and nutritional integrity due to the absence of additives and preservatives.
